FAQ

Answers to common questions about MRS LMS, our services, and how we partner with organizations to achieve learning goals.

What is a learning management system (LMS)?

An LMS is software that delivers courses, tracks learner progress, stores content, and generates reports to improve training outcomes.

Can MRS LMS integrate with our existing tools?

Yes. We support SSO, SCORM/xAPI, HRIS/CRM integrations, and API connections to fit your tech stack.

Do you offer custom course development?

Yes. Our instructional designers build tailored curricula, engaging content, and assessments aligned to your goals.

What types of reports can we access?

Enrollment, progress, completion, assessment results, competencies, and custom analytics tailored to your compliance requirements.

What is the pricing model?

We offer tiered plans and enterprise options based on users, features, data volume, and support level.
